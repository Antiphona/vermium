It is not the end

April 9, 2002
  • First
  • Previous
  • Next
  • Last

“It is not the end, but an ending. Rapid and irreversible change the consequence of a half-considered act, the nubs of new limbs rooting like seedlings, the old ones irrigated by fresh and painful furrows. There will be time again to learn to enunciate with this chorus of voices, and shine upon the exit path with clear phosgene eyes.” –Liber Vermis
